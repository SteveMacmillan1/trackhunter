How It Works

Enter a song you like and we'll give you similar song recommendations to check out.
Bookmark the ones you like then export them directly to your Spotify account.

We'll never show you the same song recommendation twice*,
and you can click an artist name to ban their songs from being recommended.

(*Must be logged in to your TrackHunter account, it's free.)

FAQ

How did I get the same recommendation twice?

Sometimes a song appears on Spotify multiple times (on different albums / releases). Our site considers them different songs because they have different Spotify track ID's.

Recommendations might also repeat for unregistered users if the browser cache is cleared.

Does banning artists affect the music style of my recommendations?

No. We just filter those artists out individually by their Spotify IDs.
